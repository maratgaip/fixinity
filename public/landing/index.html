How It Works

When you request an on-site service,
follow three easy steps.

  • Step 1: Requesting Service

    • Book your appointment and select your device.

    • Check the type of repair needed and your appointment time.

    • We ask for address where to send our technician. It can be your office building, as long as we find you.

  • Step 2: Service Details

    • Once you confirm your order we will send you a text message with appointment details.

    • On the day of your appointment we will contact you for final confirmation.

    • You can E-mail or call us with further questions.
  • Step 3: Appointment

    • Our trained technician will arrive and make the repairs as requested. Feel free to ask any questions.

    • We will perform diagnostic tests to ensure that the repairs were made properly.

    • You pay only if and when the repairs are completed.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is your service covered by a warranty?

    Yes, we stand by the quality of our parts and labor. Please inquire warranty details during your appointment.

  • Do you use original parts?

    No, this is considered a third party service. We do use high quality parts sourced from the manufacturers.

  • How long does it take to repair my phone?

    Most of our repair appointments are done within 20 minutes. We achieve such quick turn around by having pre-assembled parts ready. That's why we ask service details prior to the appointment.

  • What if my phone gets damaged during the repair?

    We recommend customers to backup their phone on a regular basis as a general practice. We do have commercial liability insurance otherwise.

  • Will I get an order confirmation?

    Yes, we will send you a text message with order details once you book an appointment. We will also contact you on the day of your appointment to confirm details.

  • Can I change my appointment?

    Yes, just send us an E-mail or Call us to reschedule.

  • Do you share my personal information?

    No, we do not. Our business model is based on customer trust and we respect it.

  • Do you accept credit cards?

    We accept major credit/debit cards as well as cash.
